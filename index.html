Privacy Policy

This application provides automated messaging functionality for Instagram Business accounts.

Information We Collect

The app may receive Instagram user identifiers, message content, and basic profile information (such as username and profile picture) as permitted by Meta APIs.

How Information Is Used

This information is used solely to process incoming messages and send automated responses through Instagram Direct Messages.

Data Storage and Retention

Data is not stored beyond what is necessary to operate the messaging automation and deliver responses.

Data Sharing

No personal data is sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their data at any time by following the instructions available at: Data Deletion Page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the method provided on this website.
